You are a senior front-end engineer and experience designer.
Build a **premium, modern, mobile-first landing page** for a Serbian B2B accounting firm **MasterMind**, tailored to the **Accuracy-Driven Manager persona**.

The page must feel:

* structured
* calm
* intelligent
* high-end professional
* visually disciplined

Use **clean semantic HTML**, **modern CSS or Tailwind**, and **minimal JS** for scroll effects & animations.
The landing page must blend **text + photography** elegantly, with hero and section images matching Persona 1’s mindset.

---

## BRAND DESIGN TOKENS

Colors (use ONLY these HEX):

* #1A1F2B (Deep Corporate Navy) → main backgrounds, header, strong text blocks
* #3A4750 (Graphite Steel) → secondary text, icon strokes
* #D9D9D9 (Soft Silver) → lines, card borders
* #0E6BA8 (Trust Blue) → CTAs, interactive highlights
* #E8F1F8 (Clean Ice White) → section backgrounds, surface layers

Typography:

* One premium sans-serif (Inter, IBM Plex Sans, or similar).
* Headings: semibold + tight tracking.
* Body: regular + relaxed line-height (supports clarity).

Spacing:

* Mobile-first
* 8pt spacing grid
* Section padding 48–72px mobile, 96px desktop

Imagery:

* Real business photography
* Neutral, cool-toned lighting
* Scenes of structured work: documents, hands organizing files, clean desks, portraits of professionals, digital dashboards
* Avoid cliché “stock smiles”; favor calm, focused expressions.

---

## UX PRINCIPLES (PERSONA 1)

This user values:

* order
* legal & financial accuracy
* predictable processes
* structured information
* minimal distraction

So the UX must:

* use **symmetry, clean grids, and simple layouts**
* show **trust cues first**
* include **real-world business photography**
* keep animations subtle & linear
* avoid overwhelming colors or flashy patterns

---

## PAGE STRUCTURE (ONE-PAGE)

Build the following sections with anchor navigation:

1. Sticky Header Navigation
2. Hero (Split layout: text + fullscreen image)
3. Trust Bar (logos or reassurance phrases)
4. “Za koga radimo” (Persona tiles with supporting images)
5. “Naše usluge” (iconographic + textual)
6. “Kako radimo” (structured process timeline with photo accents)
7. “Zašto nam veruju” (trust pillars + supporting photography)
8. “Cene i paketi” (clear, honest pricing, visually tidy)
9. FAQ accordion
10. Kontakt + Lokacije
11. Minimal footer

Smooth scroll for all anchor links.

* Sticky, deep navy (#1A1F2B) background
